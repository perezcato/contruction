Material supply

We supply a variety of materials used in construction, and the specific materials used will depend on the type of project and the desired outcome.

The construction materials include:

Concrete: This is a mixture of cement, water, and aggregates (such as sand, gravel, or crushed stone) that hardens into a strong, durable material. It is used for a wide variety of construction projects, including foundations, walls, floors, and pavements.

Steel: This is a strong and durable material that is often used in construction for structural elements such as beams, columns, and reinforcement bars. It is also commonly used for roofing, cladding, and other applications where strength and durability are required.

Wood: This natural material is used in construction for a wide variety of purposes, including framing, flooring, roofing, and decorative finishes. It is a versatile material that can be treated to enhance its strength, durability, and resistance to pests and rot.

Bricks and blocks: These are made from clay or concrete and are used for building walls, foundations, and other structural elements. They are durable and fire-resistant and can be used for both interior and exterior applications.

Glass: This material is used for windows, doors, and other building envelope elements. It can also be used for decorative purposes, such as in interior partitions and façade systems.
